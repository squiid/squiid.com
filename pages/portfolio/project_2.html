NetHope Solutions Center

Squiid is the driving creative and editorial force behind the NetHope Solutions Center – a destination for non-profits, technology vendors and private sector players to explore technology’s evolving role in development. We currently manage the site’s interactive libraries of collateral, as well as direct its strategic marketing and editorial processes. Over the past two years, Squiid has curated and developed over 400 blogs and case studies, attracting the attention of leading international organizations like GSMA, Salesforce, USAID, Microsoft and Visa. Squiid also recently led a strategic design refresh to deliver greater value to site’s target audience and funders.

To date, the site has attracted over 17,015 unique users from 175 countries. Our monthly newsletter is seen by more than 1,400 subscribers, with an on-par average open rate and an above-average click thru rate, according to MailChimp.
