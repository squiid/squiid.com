Visa

Squiid works closely with the NetHope Payment Innovations team to create and drive communications around the global Visa Innovation Grants program. Initiated in 2013, the program has issued $800k in funding to development and non-profit organizations seeking to adopt or expand the use of electronic payments in their programs and operations. Seeking to illustrate the impact of grant winners’ projects, Visa enlisted Squiid – by way of NetHope – for ongoing communications support to illuminate grantee work.

In this role, Squiid writes and designs digital content published on Visa Global Matters, NetHope.org and the Guardian. Our recent 2013 grants report and accompanying presentation reached more than 250 C-suite executives at the 2014 NetHope Member Global Summit and was later picked up and showcased on the Guardian Financial Inclusion Hub.
