MobileSpaces

Tasked with spearheading MobileSpace’s automated marketing tool, Squiid worked closely with the VP of Marketing to nurture over 15,000 leads with a combination of drip campaigns and targeted email programs. Programs seamlessly paired with Salesforce, allowing sales to identify prospective leads based on their email behaviors as well as add a human touch to the nurture process. Overall, the average open rate for Squiid’s emails was 26.1% – as compared to the 23.32% average for the Mobile industry – and the average click thru in emails was 9.1% – as compared to 3.16%, according to MailChimp.

Squiid also designed white papers and product sheets that drove website traffic, built buzz and reflected MobileSpaces’ brand.

PulseSecure acquired MobileSpaces in October 2014.
